### use
1. Self-talk notepad
2. Do-it-yourself brainstorming tools
3. A meditation technique that saturates and resets consciousness

​
### how to use
1. Think of Subjects that you are worried about, and Add Subject
2. As solilo brings up random Subject, and Add Comment
3. Respond in instinct and quickly to Subject and Add Comment
4. Keep Subjects and Comments in mind and focus to solve problems
5. Generate problem-solving ideas in the flow
6. Within 4~7 Subjects, consciousness is finally saturated and mind is overflowed, reseted

​
