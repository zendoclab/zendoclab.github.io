## Game Instructions
to expand the horizons of consciousness and improve brain function<br>

1. You can use this App in bed with eyes closed and phone in hand
2. Tap or Shake phone when it Sounds, which attemp to wake you up
3. To catch the moment of falling asleep, observe stages of consciousness while resisting falling asleep.
3. It will sound once every 15 to 30 seconds. it will quit if you do not respond to sound more than 3 times.
4. You fell asleep and app quit. Depending on phone settings, screen will turn off soon

#### relationship between hypnagogia and creativity

Hypnagogia is the state of consciousness that occurs as you are falling asleep. It is characterized by a variety of experiences, including vivid imagery, hallucinations, and a feeling of detachment from reality. Hypnagogia has been linked to creativity in a number of ways.

First, hypnagogia is a time when the brain is highly active. Theta waves, which are associated with creativity and problem-solving, are particularly high during hypnagogia. This increased brain activity may allow for new ideas and connections to be formed.

Second, hypnagogia is a time when the mind is more open to suggestion. This is because the critical filter, which is responsible for evaluating and dismissing information, is weakened during hypnagogia. This allows for more creative and unconventional ideas to be considered.

Third, hypnagogia is a time when the mind is more relaxed and focused. This is because the body is preparing for sleep and the mind is less distracted by external stimuli. This relaxed and focused state can be conducive to creativity.

Overall, hypnagogia is a state of consciousness that is conducive to creativity. The increased brain activity, the weakened critical filter, and the relaxed and focused state all contribute to the creative potential of hypnagogia.
