## Game Instructions
Transitioning to relaxation, peace, relaxation, and anti-anxiety<br>

1. Take a deep breath, Press In⟶Out button, and begin to exhale. Progress bar will advance to right
2. Exhale slowly and as long as you can. Relax whole body. Watching screen get darker has a calming effect
3. After exhaling, Press Out⟶In button and begin to inhale. Progress bar moves to left
4. length of the progress bar is a total of 60 seconds, And if you inhale for more than 0.1 seconds and exhale for more than 60 seconds, Level up when progress bar reaches all the way to right
5. You can level up more if exhalation is longer than inhalation, and in a state of deeper rest, oxygen demand is reduced so you can exhale longer


## Developer's Intention
While treating many patients suffering from anxiety disorders, I created several versions of a relaxation app to provide digital therapy in the form of a smartphone app in addition to medication, counseling, and cognitive behavioral therapy, and this is one of them​

Deep breathing definitely stabilizes your heightened sympathetic nervous system and increases your HRV, so it's the easiest, simplest, and most powerful way to regulate yourself before you go from anxiety to panic. However, breathing techniques can sometimes be boring and difficult when you don't know how to start, but this app is easy to follow and provides clear instructions and Level Up rewards to rely on

​Watching sun set in the evening makes you feel calm or euphoric, which is expressed as melancholy, and medically, it increases the blood concentration of the sleep hormone melatonin. The intention is to quickly produce a dual effect of soothing and relieving by combining deep breathing techniques with color therapy of looking at a screen that becomes increasingly black
